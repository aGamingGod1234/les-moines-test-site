# Les Moines Reference Boards

These boards are the visual contract for the fictional bakery test site. Each horizontal image defines one focused system or section.

1. `01-brand-board.png`: cobalt and porcelain identity, high-contrast wordmark, LM monogram, packaging and material language.
2. `02-hero-board.png`: full-bleed croissant hero, large editorial type, compact floating navigation and pointer-light treatment.
3. `03-counter-board.png`: five-slice pastry accordion with one expanded product, clear hover and focus affordance.
4. `04-proofing-board.png`: proofing dial and laminated dough transformation, used for the interactive craft chapter.
5. `05-atelier-board.png`: hands, flour and cobalt work surface with a calm full-bleed story composition.
6. `06-box-builder-board.png`: open pastry box with quantity controls and a decisive local demo action.
7. `07-closing-board.png`: storefront, FAQ, opening hours, fictional address and quiet footer.

## External references and decisions

- UND NY on Awwwards: contributes bread-led scroll motion and product-as-protagonist pacing, not layout or copy.
- Bernice Bakery on Awwwards: contributes playful bakery interaction and expressive transitions, restrained here for a luxury register.
- Poilâne: contributes craft authority, clear product naming and bakery-first navigation.
- BO&MIE: contributes approachable product breadth and direct shop intent.
- UI UX Pro Max editorial-grid research: contributes asymmetric hierarchy, image-led composition and scroll storytelling.

## Anti-reference rules

Do not copy another bakery. Avoid warm beige plus brass, decorative fake heritage crests, repeated cards, stacked pills, generic food stock, fake awards, unclear hover-only content and motion without a communication job.
